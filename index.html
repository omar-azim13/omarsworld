04 / Process

How I
Work

Every project follows the same four acts.

01

Imagine

Every project starts with a clear vision. What should it feel like? What problem does it solve? I sketch until the idea becomes undeniable.

02

Structure

Before writing code, I plan layout, architecture and flow. Good structure makes the build faster and the result cleaner.

03

Build

I write code, hit walls, find ways through and keep going. The actual build is where most of the real learning happens.

04

Refine

Polish turns good work into great work. I iterate on details until every interaction earns its place.
